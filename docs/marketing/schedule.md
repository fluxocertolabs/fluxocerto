# Campaign Schedule: "Adeus Planilha, Olá Futuro"

A 4-week sprint to launch FluxoCerto to the expanded market.

## Week 1: Foundation & "The Pain" (LinkedIn + Instagram)

*Focus: Agitating the problem of financial anxiety for autonomous workers.*

- **Monday:**
  - **Task:** Update Landing Page (Done).
  - **Post (LinkedIn):** "Why I built a tool to kill my own financial anxiety." (Use Prompt #3).
  - **Action:** Invite 10 close friends (Devs/Freelancers) to beta test personally.

- **Wednesday:**
  - **Post (Instagram/Stories):** Screenshot of the "Danger Zone" (red balance).
  - **Caption:** "Seu banco te avisa quando você vai entrar no cheque especial HOJE. O FluxoCerto te avisa 40 dias ANTES."

- **Friday:**
  - **Post (LinkedIn):** "The Realtor's Trap" (Use Prompt #1). Target real estate keywords.

## Week 2: The Solution & "Use Cases"

*Focus: Showing specific examples of how it solves the problem.*

- **Monday:**
  - **Post:** Video Demo (60s). Show the "Optimistic vs Pessimistic" toggle. This is the "Aha!" moment.
  - **Action:** Post on "Indie Hackers" or Brazilian dev communities (TabNews.com.br is huge).

- **Wednesday:**
  - **Post:** "Doctor's Finance" (Use Prompt #2).
  - **Ad Test (Optional):** Boost this post on Instagram targeting "Dentists" and "Doctors" interests (R$ 50 budget just to test).

- **Friday:**
  - **Feature Highlight:** "Quick Update Ritual". Emphasize it takes only 2 minutes a month. "Less time managing money, more time making money."

## Week 3: Social Proof & Scarcity

- **Monday:** Share a testimonial (even if anonymous/beta). "Stopped me from buying a car I couldn't afford yet."
- **Wednesday:** "Open Metrics". Share how many projections have been calculated. People trust transparency.
- **Friday:** "Last call for Founding Members" (if you want to add a scarcity offer, e.g., "Lock in price forever").

## Monthly Recurring Tasks (SEO)

1. **Write 1 Blog Post/Article:** "Planejamento Financeiro para Corretor de Imóveis 2026".
2. **Backlinks:** Submit to 3 SaaS directories per month.
